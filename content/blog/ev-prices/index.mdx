The majority of new cars sold in 2020 in Norway were fully electric.

Norway has led the way in EV adoption thanks to cheap hydroelectricity
bolstered by a broad array of incentives including [VAT exemption,
bus lane access, and reduced road and ferry tolls](https://wallbox.com/en_us/how-norway-became-a-global-ev-leader).
